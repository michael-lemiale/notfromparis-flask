BIO

French-American electronic duo NOT FROM PARIS is the collaboration of two brothers, Michael & Sebastian Lemiale.

The project was born in Austin, TX where the two reconnected, later embarking on a journey to blend their multi-instrumentalist roots with the vast array of sound manipulation capabilities offered by digital music production.

The result is a bold, cinematronic soundscape featuring the epic scale of a film score and their colossal, wall of sound synthesizers.

Their first NOT FROM PARIS single "crying out" is the genesis of their unique sound. They invite you to join them as they venture deeper into their cinematronic universe.
